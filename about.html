With years of professional practice, Liyang QU is a Land Surveyor at China Construction Fourth Engineering Bureau Corp., Ltd. (CCFEB), General Contracting Division. Before joining CCFEB, he earned his B.Eng. in Surveying and Mapping Engineering and M.Eng. in Civil and Hydraulic Engineering from Xiangtan University.

He specializes in 3D surface deformation field inversion, with a focus on applying space-borne SAR technologies to infrastructure monitoring, mining site rehabilitation, and urban subsidence under complex hydro-meteorological conditions.

Outside of engineering practice, he is an active technical content creator on CSDN with 19,600+ followers. In his spare time, he enjoys singing, badminton, hiking, and short-form drama series.
