QU L. Surface Deformation Monitoring and Analysis of Xiangtan Manganese Mine Based on SBAS-InSAR (in Chinese) [D]. Xiangtan University, 2024. DOI:10.27426/d.cnki.gxtdu.2024.002039. 渠立洋. 基于SBAS-InSAR的湘潭锰矿地表形变监测与分析[D]. 湘潭大学, 2024. DOI:10.27426/d.cnki.gxtdu.2024.002039.
Xiangtan Manganese Mine is known as the "Manganese Capital of China". Due to hundreds of years of mining, the local geological conditions have been severely damaged, which not only restricts local economic development, but also poses hidden dangers to the safety of local people's lives and property. To eliminate this hidden danger, the Xiangtan Manganese Mine Geological Environment Governance Demonstration Project was launched in 2012 and completed in 2018. After several years, the surface deformation affected by engineering activities has stabilized, and the secondary investigation of geological hazards has become the main problem at this stage. Compared to traditional surface deformation monitoring techniques, InSAR has advantages such as all-weather and high spatial resolution with mm-level accuracy. Therefore, this study uses time-series InSAR technology to monitor surface deformation in the Xiangtan manganese region over a long period of time, and evaluates the effectiveness of demonstration projects. The main research contents and conclusions are as follows: (1) Based on SBAS-InSAR technology, 87 Sentinel-1A SLC images from March 2017 to March 2023 were processed to obtain six-year surface deformation in the study area. Comparative verification with PS-InSAR results shows that 99.3% of monitoring points have a velocity difference within 10mm/a, proving the reliability of the calculations. (2) Spatiotemporal distribution characteristics of surface subsidence were analyzed. Two subsidence funnels formed and stabilized after 2021. Spearman correlation analysis indicates a significant positive correlation between cumulative subsidence and monthly precipitation with a one-month lag (correlation coefficient = 0.486). (3) Anthropogenic deformation factors were analyzed to evaluate the demonstration project. Most historical collapse zones have stabilized, while the newly identified Pengjiatang collapse zone remains unstable. This study provides a data basis for local redevelopment and waste mine transformation. 湘潭锰矿素有“中国锰都”之称，由于上百年的开采，当地地质条件受到严重破坏，不仅制约当地经济发展，还给当地人民的生命财产安全带来隐患。为消除这一隐患，湘潭锰矿地区矿山地质环境治理示范工程项目于2012年启动并于2018年完工。时过数年，受工程活动影响的地表形变已趋于稳定，地质灾害隐患的二次排查成为现阶段主要问题。相较于传统监测技术，InSAR具有全天时、全天候和高空间分辨率等优势，精度可达毫米级。故本研究利用时序InSAR技术对湘锰地区2017年3月至2023年3月共87景Sentinel-1A数据进行处理，对示范工程效用做出评价。主要结论如下： （1）SBAS-InSAR与PS-InSAR监测结果一致性良好，99.3%的监测点速率差不超过10mm/a，计算结果可靠。 （2）湘锰地区形成两个典型沉降漏斗，2021年前为快速发育期，2021年后进入稳定期。累计沉降量与月降水量斯皮尔曼相关系数达0.486（滞后一个月），表明降水是诱发沉降的重要自然因素。 （3）示范工程成效显著，原柴山、方竹塌陷区已基本稳定；鹅公塘塌陷区沉降趋缓；新发现的彭家塘塌陷区受固体废料填充影响仍未稳定。本研究可为矿区再开发选址及废弃矿山改造提供数据支撑与规划思路。
